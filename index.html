PGN Chess Viewer

Version 1.0, June 2012, by Timothy Ha, timh.ru.
Dedicated to the creators and users of Chess.com, the best place for playing chess online (and in mobile devices!), to learn chess and to play with humans :-)

Open a PGN file from application's menu bar or from Finder. You can also choose "Open with PGNChessViewer" in Finder's right-click menus.

This simple Mac OS X application was developed first and foremost for reading the PGN file exports of chess.com.

The engine to display chess games is PGN4Web by Paolo Casaschi, pgn4web.casaschi.net & code.google.com/p/pgn4web (GPL).
